Hello,

We are a team of researchers at the University of Washington – We are reaching out to candidates running for an elected position about the accessibility of their campaign website.

The content published at: 'www.raultorrez.com' currently contains accessibility violations for hearing and/ or visually impaired people that depend on screen reading software.

The most common violation types we found from your website are described below and our full accessibility evaluation results can be found in the attached Google Drive link. Each violation comes with an explanation of the issue and a link that you can provide to your website developer with directions for how to potentially fix the problem.

Most Common Violations:

0. link-name
	Impact: serious
	Description: Links must have discernible text
	Further Documentation: https://dequeuniversity.com/rules/axe/3.1/link-name?application=axeAPI
	Number of Pages Affected: 17
	Number of Individual Elements Affected: 51

1. color-contrast
	Impact: serious
	Description: Elements must have sufficient color contrast
	Further Documentation: https://dequeuniversity.com/rules/axe/3.1/color-contrast?application=axeAPI
	Number of Pages Affected: 17
	Number of Individual Elements Affected: 17

2. aria-roles
	Impact: critical
	Description: ARIA roles used must conform to valid values
	Further Documentation: https://dequeuniversity.com/rules/axe/3.1/aria-roles?application=axeAPI
	Number of Pages Affected: 1
	Number of Individual Elements Affected: 12

3. heading-order
	Impact: moderate
	Description: Heading levels should only increase by one
	Further Documentation: https://dequeuniversity.com/rules/axe/3.1/heading-order?application=axeAPI
	Number of Pages Affected: 4
	Number of Individual Elements Affected: 6

4. button-name
	Impact: critical
	Description: Buttons must have discernible text
	Further Documentation: https://dequeuniversity.com/rules/axe/3.1/button-name?application=axeAPI
	Number of Pages Affected: 5
	Number of Individual Elements Affected: 5
